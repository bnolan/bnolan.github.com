Why you should sell to us

Well, there's no particular reason why you should choose to sell your house to Rissa and I. If you don't already have a real estate agent, you might save some money by selling to us directly. We're mostly hoping that someone with a nice little house sees this page and would give us the opportunity to make an offer on their house before they put it up for tender through an estate agent.

Why don't we buy through an agent?

Real estate agents are lovely professional people. The only bummer with buying a house through a real estate agent is that most of the properties in our price range go for tender, instead of being sold by negotiation. It's hard to work out how much to offer in a tender process, since there is no negotiation back and forth, you instead have to guess a value and hope that you offered the most compelling offer. If you are a real estate agent and you have a property that's for sale by negotiation that you think we should look at - then we'd love to hear from you!
